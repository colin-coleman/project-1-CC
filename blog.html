Barcelona vs Villareal Preview

The teams next match is this Saturday, February 28th, where they take on Villareal at home. Barcelona are going to want to get a 2nd win in a row which would extend their lead of Real Madrid for the top of the league. This won’t be an easy match for the Culers as Villarreal have won their last two matches and sit third in the league behind Real Madrid. They last played on December 21, 2025 where a Raphina penalty and a Lamine Yamal goal would seal a 2-0 win away. Gerard Martin will be unavailable due to a suspension from accumulating too many yellow cards.

Barcelona vs Levante 3-0

Barcelona took on Levante at home today and won comfortably with a 3-0 win. The team started off strong with Bernal scoring in the 4th minute and man of the match De Jong doubling the lead later in the half. Fermin Lopez would score the final goal in the match in the 81st minute. The team had a majority of possession with 74% during the game and have now overtaken Madrid to go on top of the table. Madrid’s defeat yesterday gives Barcelona a one point lead. Gerard Martin will be unavailable next match as he got a yellow which has led to a suspension.
